About Us

PT. PELAYARAN SALAM BAHAGIA since its establishment in 1965, had been focusing in general cargo vessels serving several ports in Indonesia such as Jambi, Jakarta, Padang, Palembang, Pontianak and Surabaya. And since 1998, has actively provided marine vessels and marine related services to the oil and gas industries in Indonesia.

The company since 1998 made headway in acquiring its own fleet of Anchor Handling Tugs (AHT), Crane Barges, Accommodation Work Barges, Crew Boats, Towing Tugs and Utility Vessels to fulfill the marine support / offshore requirements of the oil and gas industries. Rooted in philosophy of safe, timely and high service quality and a firm belief that each client is a partner.

Vision

To be a leader in shipping management in Energy Industry

Mission

To provide high quality shipping supporting service through the development and innovative solution implementation with safety commitment.

To achieve highest professional standard through integrity, working quality and efficiencies and to persuade continuity for the benefit of all stake holders.
